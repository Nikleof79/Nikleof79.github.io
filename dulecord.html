DuLeCord

DuLeCord is an open source project representing a messenger with a small functionality which is enough for comfortable use. With DuLeCord you can communicate with your friends, exchange message DuLeCord is a great choice for those who are looking for a simple and effective communication tool. If you have any suggestions or would like to contribute to the development of the project, you can find it on GitHub.
